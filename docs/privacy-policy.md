# Listsurf Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Listsurf is a local-first personal list app. It does not require an account, does not include advertising, and does not use third-party analytics or tracking.

## Data Collection

Listsurf does not collect personal data from users.

Your lists, items, notes, quantities, and archive state are stored locally on your device. If you export a backup file, that file is created only at your request and remains under your control.

## Tracking

Listsurf does not track users across apps or websites.

## Network Services

Listsurf does not send your list data to a Listsurf server. The current release does not use cloud synchronization.
